About Us

Two Morrow’s Pub is a locally and family owned business that was established in 1977 by Robert Morrow with his wife Sheila Morrow. The pub closed doors in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic and months later Robert sadly passed away. Sheila offered Rocco Morabito and Spiro Papanikolatos the chance to re-open the pub. Believed to be one of the first to receive Suffolk County’s liquor licenses, located on Main Street in Patchogue, Two Morrow’s Pub prides itself on the best gourmet comfort food and drinks combined with friendly and helpful service.
